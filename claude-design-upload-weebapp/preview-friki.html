Neutral register — by subtraction

Same tokens, candy removed: no Black weight, no outlines, no Bubblegum. Calm is achieved by removing, not by adding a "serious mode."

Token-layer law.

Foil, patina, and rarity treatments apply to moments, crews, and authored cards — never to a person's card. A human being's card getting "rarer" would read as ranking people, which collides with the never-exposed-reputation and anti-humiliation rulings. This is enforced in the token layer, not left to per-screen judgment.
